Looking to learn to drive? Look no further!

I have been teaching people to drive for over 25 years, and I absolutely LOVE my job! One of the most exciting things for me is getting my driving knowledge into the heads, hands and feet of my students.

I have constantly passed the DVLA Driving Instructor Check Test at the highest grade, and am currently an A Grade ADI. I pride myself on adapting my lessons to suit my students, no matter if you are experienced, inexperienced, young, old or nervous!

In addition to teaching learners, I have taught chauffers for the Commonwealth Games, conducted driving assessments for STV and taught various members of the band Twin Atlantic!

I provide individual lessons, semi-intensive courses, Motorway lessons and Pass Plus. Don't worry if you're nervous or unsure about anything - just give me a call and can sort it out!

I look forward to welcoming you to my school, and can't wait to see you pass your test with Queens Cross!

John Reid
A Grade Approved Driving Instructor
